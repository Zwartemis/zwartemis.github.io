"We are what we create."

Liz is an up-and-coming visual designer. Her background in graphic design paired with a passion for data-driven decision making gives her an understanding of the needs of clients and users. She strives to apply cohesive and creative solutions seamlessly across a variety of media.

Strengths

  • Varied professional design experience.

    • Liz's backround in graphic design has presented the challenge of designing for a wide selection of media. From exhibit design to brand identity, catalogs to giveaways, interactive e-greetings to direct mail — she has seen and done a little of everything.

  • A hungry creative mind.

    • Liz is constantly on the hunt for her next challenge. She is curious by nature and finds a sense of satisfaction in gaining new skills. She is dedicated to finding solutions to problems, both big and small.

  • Devilish delight in the details.

    • Button wiggling a few pixels in the hover state? Slightly different spacing between printed paragraphs from page-to-page? The little things drive Liz bonkers. She works methodically to ensure everything is right the first time and won't call a project "good enough" until all those pesky ducks are neatly in a row.
